About Us

Who Are We?

We are Shark and Elena, a dynamic blend of two nationalities: Dutch and Russian.
Our journey began in the world of tropical professions, diving, and hospitality. However, the COVID-19 pandemic brought our previous endeavors to a sudden halt.
Our story started with a charming coffee haven known as Coffeehub, nestled in the heart of Bophut. With a shared vision, we set out to introduce the diverse world of coffee to Samui. In those early days, we were pioneers, offering a meticulously curated selection of 12 coffee varieties, each with its unique flavor profile and story.
Then, the unforeseen challenge of the pandemic hit. Like many others, we had to pause our operations, but our passion for exceptional coffee never wavered. It was a time for reflection, learning, and growth.
After two years of anticipation and dedication, we emerged from the shadows, reborn as Chaya Coffee. We carry forward the spirit of Coffeehub while infusing a new sense of purpose and identity. Chaya Coffee is not just a name; it's a symbol of resilience, evolution, and an unwavering commitment to bringing you the finest coffee experience.
At Chaya Coffee, we remain devoted to our craft, relentlessly pursuing excellence in every cup we brew. Join us on this journey, and let's share the love for exceptional coffee together.
Welcome to Chaya Coffee - Where Every Sip is a Story.
